8.7.5.3. Construction Requirements

(1) Sentences 8.7.4.2.(1), (2) and (4) to (11) apply to the construction of a filter bed.

(2) The lines of distribution pipe shall be evenly spaced over the surface of the filter medium to which the sanitary sewage is applied.

Note: On January 1, 2018, Sentence 8.7.5.3.(2) of Division B of the Regulation is revoked and the following substituted: (See: O. Reg. 139/17, s. 65 (1))
