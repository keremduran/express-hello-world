9.23.16.3. Attachment of Cladding to Sheathing

(1) gypsum sheathing, rigid insulationinsulation and fibreboard shall not be used for the attachment of siding materials.

(2) Nails used in attaching the materials listed in Sentence (1) shall be not less than 3.2 mm diam with a minimum head diameter of 11 mm.
