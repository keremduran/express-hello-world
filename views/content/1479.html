9.10.13.1. Closures

(1) Except as provided in Article 9.10.13.2., openings in required fire separations shall be protected with a closureconforming to Table 9.10.13.1. and shall be installed in conformance with nfpa 80, "Fire doors and Other opening Protectives", unless otherwise specified in this Part.
