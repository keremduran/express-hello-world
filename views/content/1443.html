The Ontario Building Code | Application to Houses

9.10.8.10. application to Houses

(1) Table 9.10.8.1. does not apply to a dwelling unit that has no other dwelling unit above or below it or to a dwelling unit that is not above or below another major occupancy.

Note: On July 1, 2017, Article 9.10.8.10. of Division B of the Regulation is revoked and the following substituted: (See: O. Reg. 139/17, s. 101)

9.10.8.10. Non-application to Houses

(1) Table 9.10.8.1. does not apply to houses.
