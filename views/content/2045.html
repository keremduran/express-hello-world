The Ontario Building Code | Design and Installation

9.27.13.3. design and installation

(1) The design of an exterior insulation finish system shall comply with CAN/ulc-S716.3, "exterior Insulation and Finish Systems (EIFS) – design Application".

(2) The installation of an exterior insulationinsulation finish system shall comply with CAN/ulc-S716.2, "exterior Insulation and Finish Systems (EIFS) – installation of EIFS Components and water Resistive Barrier".
