9.25.2.2. insulationinsulation Materials

(1) Except as required in Sentence (2), thermal insulationinsulation shall conform to the requirements of,

(a) CAN/cgsb-51.25-M, "Thermal insulationinsulation, Phenolic, Faced",

(b) cgsb 51-GP-27M, "Thermal insulationinsulation, Polystyrene, Loose Fill",

(c) CAN/ulc-S701, "Thermal insulationinsulation, Polystyrene, Boards and pipe Covering",

(d) CAN/ulc-S702 "Mineral Fibre Thermal insulationinsulation for Buildings",

(e) CAN/ulc-S703, "Cellulose Fibre insulationinsulation (CFI) for buildings",

(f) CAN/ulc-S704, "Thermal insulationinsulation, Polyurethane and Polyisocyanurate, Boards, Faced",

(g) CAN/ulc-S705.1, "Thermal insulationinsulation – Spray Applied Rigid Polyurethane Foam, Medium Density – Material – Specification", or

(h) CAN/ulc-S706, "wood Fibre Thermal insulationinsulation for Buildings".

(2) Theflame-spread rating requirements contained in the standards listed in Sentence (1) shall not apply.

(3) insulationinsulation in contact with the ground shall be inert to the action of soil and water and be such that its insulative properties are not significantly reduced by moisture.

(4) Type 1 expanded polystyrene insulationinsulation as described in CAN/ulc-S701, "Thermal insulationinsulation, Polystyrene, Boards and pipe Covering", shall not be used as roof insulationinsulation applied above the roofing membrane.
