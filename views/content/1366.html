The Ontario Building Code | Application

9.9.4.1. application

(1) Except as provided in Articles 9.9.4.4. and 9.9.4.6., this Subsection applies to the fire protection of all exits except exits serving a single dwelling unit.

Note: On July 1, 2017, Sentence 9.9.4.1.(1) of Division B of the Regulation is amended by striking out "a single dwelling unit" at the end and substituting "an individualdwelling unit". (See: O. Reg. 139/17, s. 91)
