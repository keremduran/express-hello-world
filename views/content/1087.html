7.5.5.5. Provision for Future installations

(1) Where provision is made for a fixture to be installed in the future, the drainage system andventing system shall be sized accordingly and provision made for the necessary future connections.

(2) Except as required in Sentence 7.5.7.7.(2), where a plumbing system is installed in a building, every storey in which plumbingis or may be installed, including the basement of a single family dwelling, shall have extended into it or passing through it a vent pipe that is at least 1 ½ in. in size for the provision of future connections.

Note: On July 1, 2017, Sentence 7.5.5.5.(2) of Division B of the Regulation is revoked and the following substituted: (See: O. Reg. 139/17, s. 54)

(2) Except as required in Sentence 7.5.7.7.(2), where a plumbing system is installed in a house, every storey in which plumbingis or may be installed, including the basement of thehouse, shall have extended into it or passing through it a vent pipe that is at least 1 ½ in. in size for the provision of future connections.
